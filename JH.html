Club Activities


Students are required to join SPAS and pick what they would like . They choices are Volleyball, Basketball, Journalism,Arnis, Photography, Boy Scout & Girl Scout, Science club, Math Club, and English Club.
The assigned time for their SPAS is after their classes from 4:15 - 5:00 pm

Note: For those who are interested you can go to the Principal Office to know if there are still available spots for students
